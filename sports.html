The National Basketball Association is a men's basketball league in North America, composed of 30 teams. The sport of basketball was created by Dr. James Naismith in the late 19th century when he was just a physical education instructor at a YMCA in Springfield, Massachusetts
The National Football League is a professional American football league consisting of 32 teams, divided equally between the National Football Conference and the American football conference. Football was created by Walter Camp. Walter camp evolved the game of rugby into American football.
Major League Basbeall is a professional baseball league that consists of 29 teams in the United States and 1 team in Canada. There are 2 leagues in the MLB, 15 teams in the American League and 15 teams in the National League. The game of baseball was created by Abner Doubleday and the current commisssioner of the MLB is Robert Manfred.
